---
title: Customer
description: Customers are at the heart of the delivery process. They receive orders from or return orders back to shops.
---

## Fields

### `id`: `number`
**Attributes:** Unique  
A unique identifier assigned to each customer.

### `name`: `string`

The full name of the customer.

### `phone_number`: `string`
**Attributes:** Unique
  
The customer's primary phone number, including the country code.

### `secondary_phone_number`: `string`

An alternative phone number for the customer, also including the country code.

### `email`: `string`

The customer's email address.

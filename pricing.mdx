## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change plans anytime?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

**What happens if I exceed my API limit?**
We'll notify you before you reach your limit. If exceeded, requests will be temporarily throttled until the next billing cycle.

**Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**
Yes, save 20% when you pay annually on any plan.
